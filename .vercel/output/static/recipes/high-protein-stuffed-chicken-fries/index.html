High-Protein Stuffed Chicken Fries

Crispy, cheesy stuffed chicken fries made with a chicken mince coating and melty mozzarella center. High in protein and air-fried for an easy viral-style dinner or snack.

Jump to Recipe 3/18/2026

Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These stuffed chicken fries are crispy on the outside, cheesy in the center, and packed with protein. They feel like comfort food but are made with simple ingredients and cook quickly in the air fryer.

Simple Ingredients

Chicken mince creates the outer coating, while mozzarella gives each fry a melty center. A breadcrumb and parmesan coating adds extra crunch and helps take the texture to the next level.

Best Ways to Enjoy It

Serve these hot with your favorite dip like marinara, ranch, or spicy mayo. They work well as a high-protein snack, an easy dinner, or a fun appetizer for sharing.

Dry Ingredients

  • Salt, to taste Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste Black pepper, to taste
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs (optional, for coating) 60ml breadcrumbs (optional, for coating)
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan 30ml grated parmesan

Wet Ingredients

  • 320g (11 oz) chicken mince 320g (312g) chicken mince
  • 1 large egg, beaten (optional, for coating) 1 large egg, beaten (optional, for coating)

Optional

  • 80g (2.8 oz) light mozzarella, cut into 4 sticks 80g (79g) light mozzarella, cut into 4 sticks
  • Cooking spray Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 385°F / 195°C.
  2. Season the chicken mince with salt and black pepper and mix well.
  3. Divide the chicken mixture into 4 equal portions.
  4. Flatten one portion in your hand and place a mozzarella stick in the center.
  5. Wrap the chicken around the mozzarella and shape it into a fry-like stick.
  6. Repeat with the remaining chicken and mozzarella.
  7. For a crispier coating, dip each stick into beaten egg and roll in breadcrumbs mixed with parmesan.
  8. Lightly spray the coated chicken fries with cooking spray.
  9. Place the chicken fries in the air fryer in a single layer.
  10. Air fry for 10–15 minutes until golden brown and fully cooked through.
  11. Serve hot while the cheese is still melted inside.

Tips

  • Seal the chicken tightly around the mozzarella so the cheese does not leak during cooking.
  • Use the egg wash and breadcrumb coating if you want a crispier finish.
  • Do not overcrowd the air fryer basket or the fries may steam instead of crisp.

Variations

  • Use ground turkey instead of chicken if preferred.
  • Add garlic powder, paprika, or chili flakes to the chicken mixture for more flavor.
  • Skip the breadcrumbs for a lower-carb version.

Storage

  • Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
  • Reheat in the air fryer for a few minutes to bring back the crisp texture.

FAQ

Can I make these without breadcrumbs?

Yes. They will still cook well, but the outside will be less crispy.

Why is the cheese leaking out?

Make sure the chicken fully seals around the mozzarella with no cracks or thin spots.

Can I bake these instead?

Yes. Bake at 400°F / 200°C for about 18–20 minutes or until fully cooked through.
