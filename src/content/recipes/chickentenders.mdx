# Air Fryer Chicken Tenders

These tenders are the perfect “I want fried food but I’m watching my macros” solution. Crunchy outside, tender inside, and packed with protein thanks to a smart coating mix.

## Ingredients (serves 4)
- 1.5 lbs chicken breast tenders (or sliced breasts)
- 1 cup almond flour (or crushed pork rinds for extra crunch)
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 large eggs (for egg wash)
- Cooking spray (avocado oil preferred)

## Instructions
1. Pat the chicken tenders dry with paper towels.
2. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs.
3. In another bowl, mix almond flour, Parmesan, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
4. Dip each tender in egg, then coat thoroughly in the dry mix. Press to adhere.
5. Preheat air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
6. Spray the basket and place tenders in a single layer (don’t overcrowd).
7. Air fry 10–12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and internal temp reaches 165°F.
8. Rest 2 minutes before serving.

## Serving Suggestions
- Pair with sugar-free buffalo sauce or a Greek yogurt ranch dip.
- Serve over a big salad for volume eating or with roasted veggies.
