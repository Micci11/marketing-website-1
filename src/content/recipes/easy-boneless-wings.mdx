## Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This recipe is all about speed and simplicity. Using store-bought chicken tenders means you skip the mess of breading and frying while still getting crispy boneless wings in minutes.

## Simple Ingredients

All you need are chicken tenders and your favorite sauce. Everything else is optional depending on how you want to serve them.

## Best Ways to Enjoy Them

These wings work with almost any sauce. Buffalo and BBQ are classic options, but sweet chili sauce creates a great Asian-inspired version.

## Optional Serving Idea

If you're making the sweet chili version like I do, serving the wings over a bowl of rice turns them into a quick Asian-style meal.
