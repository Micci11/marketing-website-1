ingredientsDry:
  - "½ tsp seasoning (or salt and black pepper, to taste)"

ingredientsWet:
  - "2 large eggs"
  - "½ cup (120g) cottage cheese"
  - "½ tbsp chili oil (plus extra for topping, optional)"

optionalIngredients:
  - "½ small shallot, finely minced"
  - "Fresh chives, chopped"

instructions:
  - "Preheat a skillet over medium heat."
  - "Add the cottage cheese and chili oil to the pan."
  - "Cook for 1–2 minutes until the cottage cheese softens and becomes creamy."
  - "Crack the eggs into a bowl or directly into the pan, keeping the yolks intact."
  - "Add the seasoning and minced shallots."
  - "Cover the pan and cook for 2–3 minutes until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still soft."
  - "Remove from heat and garnish with chives and optional extra chili oil."
  - "Serve immediately."

tips:
  - "Let the cottage cheese melt slightly before adding the eggs for the best texture."
  - "Covering the pan helps cook the tops of the eggs evenly."
  - "Keep the heat medium to avoid burning the dairy."

variations:
  - "Use heavy cream instead of cottage cheese for a richer version."
  - "Add spinach or mushrooms for extra volume."
  - "Top with parmesan or feta for extra flavor."

## Why You’ll Love This Recipe

These eggs are creamy, slightly crispy on the edges, and packed with flavor. The cottage cheese adds extra protein and richness while keeping it balanced.

## Simple Ingredients

Eggs and cottage cheese form a high-protein base, while chili oil adds heat and depth. Simple toppings like chives and shallots bring freshness and contrast.

## Best Ways to Enjoy It

Serve straight from the pan with toast or wrap it in a tortilla. It’s perfect for a quick high-protein breakfast or an easy savory snack.
