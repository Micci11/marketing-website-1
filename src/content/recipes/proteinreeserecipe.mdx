This recipe has been everywhere on Instagram, usually filmed in perfect lighting
with zero mention of texture or cleanup.

### The result
Surprisingly, this one actually works — **but only** if you blend it longer than
most videos admit.

### What went wrong in the viral version
Most versions under-blend the cottage cheese, which leads to a grainy texture and
tearing when you try to cook it.

### How to make it work
- Use a high-speed blender
- Blend until completely smooth
- Let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking

### Final verdict
Would I make this again? **Yes.**  
Would I trust the original video instructions? **Absolutely not.**
