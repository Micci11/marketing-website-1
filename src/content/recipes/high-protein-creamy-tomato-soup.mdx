ingredientsDry:
  - "Garlic powder, to taste"
  - "Onion powder, to taste"
  - "Mixed herbs, to taste"
  - "Salt and pepper, to taste"
  - "20g cornflour"

ingredientsWet:
  - "1 cup (240 ml) marinara sauce"
  - "1 cup (240 g) cottage cheese"
  - "1 cup (240 ml) broth"
  - "Splash of water (to mix with cornflour)"

optionalIngredients:
  - "Fresh chilli, chopped"

instructions:
  - "In a blender, combine the marinara sauce, cottage cheese, broth, garlic powder, onion powder, mixed herbs, salt, and pepper."
  - "Blend until completely smooth."
  - "In a small bowl, mix the cornflour with a splash of water to create a slurry."
  - "Pour the blended mixture into a pot over medium heat."
  - "Add the cornflour slurry and stir well."
  - "Cook for 5–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth and creamy."
  - "Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot."

tips:
  - "Blend thoroughly for the smoothest texture—this removes any cottage cheese graininess."
  - "Add extra seasoning if needed—this recipe benefits from bold flavor."
  - "Stir consistently once heating to avoid lumps from the cornflour."

variations:
  - "Add cooked ground turkey or chicken to turn it into a full pasta sauce."
  - "Use vegetable broth for a lighter flavor or chicken broth for more depth."
  - "Add parmesan on top for extra richness."

storage:
  - "Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days."
  - "Reheat gently on the stove, adding a splash of water if it thickens too much."

## Why You’ll Love This Recipe

This sauce is ridiculously easy to make and adds a big protein boost to any meal. It’s creamy, flavorful, and feels way more indulgent than it actually is.

## Simple Ingredients

Cottage cheese blends into the marinara to create a smooth, high-protein base, while broth helps thin it out and keep it balanced. Simple seasonings bring everything together.

## Best Ways to Enjoy It

Use it over pasta, with chicken, as a dipping sauce, or even as a base for other recipes. It’s super versatile and easy to batch for the week.
